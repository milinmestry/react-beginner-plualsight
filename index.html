Game Help

The game is simple, used all the numbers from the tray to match the number of stars. When the page loads it will display the star(s) and you have to click on number(s) for the count of star(s). For Example, if the stars appears 7, and you can select number 7 or numbers 5 + 2 or 4 + 3 or 6 + 1; depending upong how many Numbers are available in the numbers tray. Keep in mind that once you matched the number(s) with star(s), then those number(s) is/are marked as used, and will be disabled in the numbers tray.

You can use 5 refreshes to see the next stars on the page, this may help you to win the game when you are out of certains numbers.
